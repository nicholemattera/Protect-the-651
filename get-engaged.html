Get Engaged

Join Signal Groups

Get out into your local community, meet your neighbors, and through grass root efforts join your neighborhood signal group. If you need help setting up Signal, check out our Signal Support page.

Distribute Resources

Get friends and family together and have a zine folding party. You can find a few helpful zines and resources throughout our pages. Once you have them check with local businesses and shops if they need any resources. Little Free Libraries are a great place to distribute zines and whistles.
