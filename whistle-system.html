Why a whistle?

  • It's a simple tool
  • It's loud, recognizable, and impossible to ignore
  • It's faster than trying to reach responders through social media

Whistle Codes

  • 3 short bursts = ICE is in the area
  • 3 long blasts = ICE is abducting someone
