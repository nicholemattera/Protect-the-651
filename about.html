Oh, hell no.

On December 1st, the federal government launched Operation Metro Surge, deploying ICE agents and other federal enforcement officers to Saint Paul and the greater Twin Cities metro.

Since that time, our immigrant communities have been at heightened risk of abduction, detention, and deportation. The fear is palpable—and rightly so.

No one should have to face it alone.

That's why community members across the east metro are building hyper-local, decentralized protection teams and infrastructure. We're patrolling our communities, reporting on ICE activity in real-time, and training more responders to grow our capacity.

The mission: To build solidarity and actively protect our families, friends, and neighbors everywhere they live and work.
